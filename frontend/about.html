WHAT IT CAN DO

01
AI AGILE PLAN GENERATOR
Instantly generates full sprint plans, epics, user stories, task breakdowns and risk analysis using Groq AI.
POST /project/create
02
AI RISK PREDICTOR
Predicts project risk score based on overdue tasks, workload levels, sprint velocity and retrospective data.
GET /risk-predictor/{id}
03
WORKLOAD ANALYZER
Distributes tasks evenly across team members and detects overloaded developers automatically.
GET /workload/{id}
04
SPRINT RETROSPECTIVE
Auto-generates what went well, what went wrong and improvement suggestions for every sprint.
GET /retrospective/{id}
05
SPRINT ANALYTICS
Analyzes sprint efficiency, stories per sprint, average velocity and provides an efficiency indicator.
GET /analytics/{id}
06
RISK EXTRACTOR
Extracts high, medium and low risks from the AI plan with calculated risk scores and counts.
GET /risk/{id}
07
TASK TRACKER
Extracts and monitors all project tasks with assigned roles, estimated effort and story mapping.
GET /task/{id}
08
DASHBOARD METRICS
Shows total stories, tasks, estimated hours, team workload and overall risk score at a glance.
GET /dashboard/{id}
